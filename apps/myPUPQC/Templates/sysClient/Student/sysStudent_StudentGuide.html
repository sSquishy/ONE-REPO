Freshmen Guide

What should I look For?

1. You should check your schedule. You can see it in your PUPSIS Account.
2. You should check your room assignment. You can see it in your PUPSIS Account.
3. You should check who your professors are. You can see it in your PUPSIS Account.

Freshmen Essentials

1. Download the PUP Student Handbook and read it.
2. Bag
3. Tumbler for water (optional)
4. Wallet
5. Ballpen
6. Yellow paper
7. Binder for your notes (optional)
8. Smart phone
9. Other essentials will depend on your professors' requirements. You can ask them.

How to Request PUPWebmail

Send an email to webmail@pup.edu.ph with an attached copy of your PUP ID or any valid government ID.
Due to the volume of email requests we receive daily from all PUP students, faculty, employees, and even outside clients, our response may take longer than expected.

Processing Time:
1. Simple transactions within three (3) working days,
2. Seven (7) working days for complex transactions, and
3. Twenty (20) working days for highly technical transactions.

Reference: PUP Webmail

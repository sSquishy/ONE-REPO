Frequently Asked Question

Admission Process ▾
The Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP) offers a streamlined admission process for both undergraduate and graduate programs. Applicants must submit their requirements online through the PUP iApply portal and wait for the release of the list of qualified applicants.
Scholarship Programs ▾
PUP provides various scholarship programs to support financially challenged students. These include the PUP Academic Scholarship, PUP Student Assistance Program, and external scholarships sponsored by government and private organizations.
Online Learning Platforms ▾
PUP utilizes online learning platforms such as PUP iLearn and PUP Open University to provide flexible and accessible education to students. These platforms offer a wide range of courses and resources for both synchronous and asynchronous learning.
Student Organizations ▾
PUP encourages students to join various student organizations to enhance their leadership skills and social networks. These organizations include academic clubs, cultural groups, and advocacy-based organizations.
